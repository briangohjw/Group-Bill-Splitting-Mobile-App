ReShare Terms of Use (Spotify)
ReShare offers services for recurring payments and bill-splitting to group subscription plans.

The cost of the Subscription Fee will be borne by all Members equally, subject to ReShare’s Terms of Use.

Where one member has an insufficient bank balance to pay for his share of the Subscription Fee (“Non-Paying Member”), the member who created the group (“Group Leader”) will bear the cost of the Non-Paying Member’s fee temporarily.

Where the Group Leader has borne the cost of the Non-Paying Member’s fee temporarily, monies owed to the Group Leader will be charged to the Non-Paying Member the subsequent month, in fulfilment of the Group Leader’s share of the Subscription Fee.

Members also have the option to settle monies owed outside of Paypal, subject to the applicable laws.

ReShare and Group Leaders reserve the right to kick any Member out of his/her group.

The Subscription Plan will automatically terminate at the end of the period when the Group Leader has an insufficient bank balance to fulfill the Subscription Fee.

In the event where the Group Leader wishes to leave the group, he/she can appoint another member to take on the role of “Group Leader”, subject to acceptance by that member.

A convenience fee of 1% of the Subscription Fee will be shared equally among Members.
